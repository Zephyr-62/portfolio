About Me


I'm a computer science engineer that wants to take part in the art industry and make a living out of game development.

I consider myself to be a quiet and reflective person, but I also enjoy exciting plans and going on adventures!

The Games I Love

I recently tried to list every game I've ever played, and so far my list includes about 200 games. My favourite genres are indie and puzzle games. I love the freedom of expression these kind of games have, and I quite enjoy logic challenges, such as the ones posed in Baba is You.

I could never pick which one of all of these is my favourite game, but a contender list goes as follows:

  • Outer Wilds
  • The Last of Us
  • Tunic
  • The Witness
  • Satisfactory
  • The Legend of Zelda (TP)
  • Baba is You
  • The Talos Principle
  • A Short Hike
  • Hollow Knight
  • Celeste
  • Dark Souls III

My Hobbies

Social Life

I love nature and having long walks and hikes with my friends. I'm used to walking away from the main path so that I feel like I'm exploring where no one else has been before, and I usually drag people around with me. I also enjoy spending time with my people by watching movies or playing games together, but my favourite activity is deep talks with the people that is closest to me.

Artistic Expression

I practice many forms of art. The most notorious one is video games, I also play music and do some crafts, like 3D printed figurines. I started playing piano when I was 12, and I also know a couple of songs on the guitar. I like to show my passion by wearing geek clothes with designs from games like HK, TLOU, or ghibli movies. I also recently took part in a workshop from Moonhood, where I discovered a new hobby: sculpting.

Personal Projects

The last type of activity I engage with in my free time is working on personal projects. This is another way of expressing how I love puzzle games, since every new project poses new logical challenges! These involve both artistic and software projects. The one I'm most proud of is the free Unity3D asset I worked on called Advanced Editor Tools.

Furthermore, right now I'm working on the first project of Numae Games as a tool developer and level designer.
