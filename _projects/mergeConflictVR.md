summary: "Exploration of VR sense of space <b><i>without</b></i> using virtual locomotion systems (e.g. joysticks). Walk in-game by walking in real life."
shortDescription: "This prototype of a game makes use of stationary portals as a means of redirected walking within a non-euclidean environment. That is to say: all movement in virtual reality is real-world movement. To play this game you require a 4x4 meter space free of obstacles. <br><br> Work your way around this space altering environment to roll back changes made to <b>scale</b>, <b>time</b>, and <b>perspective</b>. Each of this represents a puzzle branch of which each team member had ownership. I was personally in charge of the <i>scale</i> puzzle branch. I also worked on other important systems like the interaction with props and the out of bounts system, along with tools to easily map our scenes to the real world play space."
