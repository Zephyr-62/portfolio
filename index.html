Zote The Mighty Fangame

A Hollow Knight Fan Video Game DEMO

This was my first Unity project I worked on. It was made during my bachelors in a Game Development Course in 4 months. I worked in a team of 3 and we designed a full world and story around it. The demo we managed to deliver only contains a few mechanics and enemies, but quite a few systems necessary to start with the level design. We have a YouTube trailer here, and an earlt gameplay overview here.

I personally worked on the project management; the camera system which detects if an area has been discovered or not, and is bounded to an area collider; I worked on the mechanics for the main character, that is, movement and special abilities; scene transition system; interaction system; and finally I worked on a few sprite animations.
